Whether you are looking to buy or sell, National Lift Equipment is the Construction Equipment Specialist.

Established in 1990 in Bucks County, PA, the heart of the East Coast corridor, National Lift Equipment is conveniently located close to all major East Coast thorofare’s. We have developed a reputation for reliable new and used Aerial Lifts and Material Handling Equipment. Our locally owned status ensures attention to quality and overall customer satisfaction.

Here at National Lift Equipment, we offer equipment from the industry's leading manufacturers such as JLG, Skytrak, Genie, Caterpillar, Skyjack, JCB, Haulotte, Nifty, MEC, Gehl and many others.

Quality, service, pricing and selection, is what sets National Lift Equipment apart from the competition.
